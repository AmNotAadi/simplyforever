⚠️ Important: Server is Whitelisted

🛡️ Why We're Whitelisted

Due to the "forever" nature of our server, we maintain a whitelist to ensure only committed players join our community. This helps protect our persistent world and maintain a high-quality player base.

📋 How to Get Whitelisted

1

Join Our Discord

First, join our Discord community to connect with staff and other players.

Join Discord
2

Read #whitelist-info

Check the #whitelist-info channel in our Discord for detailed application requirements and guidelines.

3

Submit Application

Follow the instructions in #whitelist-info to submit your application for review.

4

Wait for Approval

Applications are typically processed within 24 hours. You'll receive a notification when you're whitelisted!

⏰ Processing Time

All whitelist applications are processed within 24 hours. We understand you're excited to join, and we work quickly to get new players into the game!
