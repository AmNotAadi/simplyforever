Official Server Rules

  1. No Hacking or Cheating

    Void hacking activities, including x-ray, baritone, seed cracking, and unauthorized client modifications. Use only vanilla Minecraft or approved mods.

  2. No Duplication

    Duplication is strictly prohibited, with the exception of TNT and Carpet duplication. All other forms of item duplication are not allowed.

  3. No Lag Machines

    Refrain from creating lag machines or engaging in actions that degrade server performance. This includes excessive redstone contraptions and mob farms.

  4. Be Respectful

    Maintain respectful interactions‚Äîharassment, bullying, slander, or personal attacks are unacceptable. Treat others as you would like to be treated.

  5. Keep Content Appropriate

    Ensure that all content is suitable for all audiences‚Äîavoid NSFW, gore, or offensive material in public areas. This includes builds, signs, and chat messages.

  6. No Unnecessary Pinging

    Do not ping roles or users without a legitimate reason. Request assistance courteously and avoid spam pinging.

  7. No Unauthorized Advertising

    Advertising is only permitted when directly related to Simply Forever and has received prior approval. No external server or service promotion.

  8. Griefing Guidelines

    Griefing player bases is allowed, but do not grief public warps, spawn, or place random blocks around the server. Respect community areas.

  9. No Bug Exploitation

    Exploiting bugs or glitches is strictly prohibited‚Äîreport any issues to the staff immediately. Help us maintain a fair gaming environment.

  10. Follow Staff Directions

    Adhere to the directions provided by staff and respect their decisions at all times. Staff decisions are final and made in the best interest of the community.
