Land Claims

Protect your builds and manage your claimed areas

üè† Land Claims System

Our land claims system allows you to protect your builds, farms, and areas from griefing and unwanted modifications. Claim blocks are earned through playtime, giving active players more space to protect their creations.

Claim Block System

You earn 150 claim blocks every hour you play on the server. These blocks can be used to create protected areas where only you and trusted players can build, break blocks, or interact with containers.

‚ö° Land Claim Commands

/abandonclaim

Deletes the claim you're currently standing in. This will remove all protection from that area and return the claim blocks to your pool.

Example: /abandonclaim
‚ö†Ô∏è Warning: This action cannot be undone. Make sure you want to remove protection from this area.

/claimexplosions

Toggles whether explosions are allowed in your claim. Useful for TNT-based builds or if you want to allow creeper explosions for natural terrain modification.

Example: /claimexplosions
üí° Tip: Disabling explosion protection can be useful for TNT cannons or controlled demolition projects.

/trust | /t

Gives another player permission to edit in your claim. They can break blocks, place blocks, and modify the terrain within your protected area.

Example: /trust Steve or /t Alex
üí° Tip: Use this for trusted friends or team members who need to help with building projects.

/untrust | /ut

Revokes any permissions granted to a player in your claim. They will no longer be able to modify anything within your protected area.

Example: /untrust Steve or /ut Alex
üí° Tip: Use this if you need to remove access for someone who previously had permission.

/containertrust | /ct

Gives a player permission to use your buttons, levers, beds, crafting gear, containers, and animals within your claim. They cannot break or place blocks.

Example: /containertrust Steve or /ct Alex
üí° Tip: Perfect for allowing friends to use your farms, storage systems, or automated contraptions without full building access.

/abandonallclaims

Deletes all of your claims across the server. This will remove protection from all areas you've claimed and return all claim blocks to your pool.

Example: /abandonallclaims
‚ö†Ô∏è Warning: This action cannot be undone and will affect ALL your claims. Use with extreme caution.

üîß How Land Claims Work

üéØ

Creating Claims

Claims are created automatically when you place a claim block or use the claim wand. The system will protect the area around you based on your available claim blocks.

üõ°Ô∏è

Protection Features

Protected areas prevent other players from breaking blocks, placing blocks, using containers, or interacting with redstone components unless they have permission.

‚è∞

Claim Block Earning

You earn 150 claim blocks for every hour you're actively playing on the server. These accumulate over time, allowing you to protect larger areas.

üë•

Permission System

Grant different levels of access to other players: full trust for building, container trust for using facilities, or no access for complete protection.

üí∞ Claim Block Management

Earning Claim Blocks

Rate: 150 claim blocks per hour of active play

Accumulation: Blocks are earned continuously while online

Maximum: No limit on total claim blocks you can earn

Efficient Usage Tips

  • Start Small: Begin with smaller claims and expand as needed
  • Strategic Placement: Place claims to maximize protected area
  • Regular Review: Remove claims from areas you no longer need
  • Team Coordination: Work with others to avoid overlapping claims

Need More Claim Blocks?

If you need additional claim blocks beyond what you earn through playtime, you can purchase more through the server store or contact staff for assistance.
