NPC Shopkeepers

Each NPC specializes in different types of items. Some focus on building materials, others on food, tools, or rare items. Each has their own inventory and pricing structure.

Buying Items

Right-click on an NPC to open their shop interface. Browse their inventory, select items you want to buy, and confirm your purchase. The cost will be deducted from your balance.

üì¶

Selling Items

Most NPCs also buy items from players. Place items in the selling interface to see their buy price. This is a great way to earn money from excess materials.

Dynamic Pricing

Prices can fluctuate based on supply and demand. Popular items may become more expensive, while abundant items may become cheaper. Keep an eye on market trends!

Best Times to Shop

Visit shops during off-peak hours when fewer players are online. This can sometimes result in better prices and more available inventory.

üìä

Compare Prices

Don't buy from the first shop you see! Check multiple NPCs for the same item - prices can vary significantly between different shopkeepers.

üí°

Sell Smart

Process raw materials before selling. For example, sell cooked food instead of raw meat, or sell planks instead of logs for better profit margins.

üîÑ

Market Cycles

Pay attention to what other players are building or needing. High-demand items often have better selling prices, while oversupplied items may sell for less.
